The
Filmmaker

Ayman Hassdo is an award-winning director and cinematographer whose work explores memory, identity, and the places where they collide.

With a rigorous visual language and a deeply humanist perspective, Hassdo has spent over a decade making films that refuse easy answers — drawing on landscape as character and silence as language.
