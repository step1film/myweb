03 — About

The Filmmaker

Alejandro Rivera is an award-winning Argentine director and cinematographer whose work explores the intersection of memory, identity, and landscape.

Born in Buenos Aires and trained at the Centro de Investigación Cinematográfica, Rivera has spent two decades crafting films that live at the border between the personal and the political. His visual language draws from the long tradition of Latin American magical realism, filtered through a rigorous, contemplative lens.

His features and shorts have screened at over 80 international festivals, received 14 major awards, and been acquired for distribution in 40+ countries. Rivera also teaches advanced directing at the Universidad del Cine.
